There are days when we get lazy and that is when we create things like MAGGI Noodles Easy-Peasy. But, if you think we compromised on this one, you are wrong â€“ you can never go wrong with your favourite MAGGI Noodles

ingredient

  • MAGGI Rich Tomato Ketchup
  • Oil
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Tomato (Finely Chopped)
  • Onion (Finely Chopped)
  • Peas
  • Salt
  • Kasuri Methi

Instruction

  • Step 1 :Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
  • Step 2: Heat the oil and then roast the cumin seeds in it. Then, toss in the tomatoes, the onions and cook them well. Add the peas and the MAGGI Tomato ketchup, give it a stir, and let them cook for a while!
  • step 3: Cook one pack of MAGGI Masala Noodles (just follow the instructions on the pack!). Add the kasoori methi while cooking noodles.Transfer the noodles into a bowl.
  • step 4:Put the cumin-tomato-onion mix right on top of the MAGGI noodles and you are ready to serve the easy peasy fun!
