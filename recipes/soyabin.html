Description

Soya Bean Curry Recipe with step by step pictures. This is the popular gravy dish which is rich in protein and it tastes great with rice or roti.

ingredient

  • Soy Beans - 1 cup dried beans
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Cumin Seeds / Jeerakam - 1.5 tsp
  • Garlic - 4 fat cloves crushed
  • Chilli Powder -2 tsp or to taste
  • Turmeric Powder / Manjal Podi - 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala Powder - 1 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves a handful

instruction

  • step 1:Wash and soak soy beans overnight. next day drain them and add it to a pressure cooker
  • step 2:Now cover with water. Pressure cook for 4 whistle, simmer the pan and cook on a low heat for 10 mins.
  • step 3:Turn off the heat and let the steam go all by itself.Open the cooker and set aside the dal along with cooking liquid.
  • step 4:Heat oil in a pan. add in cumin seeds and crushed garlic. Saute for a min.
  • step 5:Add in chilli powder, turmeric and garam masala powder. Saute well in the oil.
  • step 6:Add in the cooked dal along with the cooking liquid.
  • step 7:Now add some salt. Bring this whole thing to a boil. simmer till the curry reaches the right thickness.
  • step 8:Add coriander leaves and mix well.
  • step 9:Serve with rice or roti.
